What Is "Best Of PGH"?   Pittsburgh City Paper's "Best of PGH" issue aims to celebrate the most influential, most underrated, most dynamic, and all-around best businesses, creators, events, and leaders in our community. We team up with our readers to unearth hidden gems and reward old faithfuls, bringing you a jam-packed issue that serves as your key to the city all year round.
