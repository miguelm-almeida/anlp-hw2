Launched by the PDP with support from the City of Pittsburgh, Allegheny County, and Downtown business and property owners, this pilot program is bringing temporary restroom facilities to accessible locations within the city center, as part of a series of measures to improve cleanliness and quality of life in Downtown, while advancing the long-term goal of establishing more permanent public restrooms in the Golden Triangle.
