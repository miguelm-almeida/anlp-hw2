The Monteverdi  Society recognizes opera lovers who have deepened their commitment to Pittsburgh Opera and invested in its future by providing a legacy gift of any size in their estate plans.

Named after the father of the structure of opera as we know it today, the Monteverdi Society ensures the all-encompassing art form of opera for future generations.

We are grateful for the members of the Monteverdi Society and for the lasting mark they make on the opera world and on our community.

Reduce your tax burden and ensure that more of your assets are distributed in accordance with your wishes - become a member of the Monteverdi Society today.
