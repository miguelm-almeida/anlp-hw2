Looking for a student, senior, or other discount? Listed below are the active promotions and discounts available.

Any group of 6 or more is eligible for discounted group tickets. Please see our Group Tickets page, or email our Group Sales Manager Wendy Parkulo, for questions and more information.

Active duty and retired military are eligible for 20% off tickets to all Pittsburgh Opera productions. These tickets are available online with the promo code POMILITARY, at the Box Office, or by calling 412-456-6666. These tickets will be held at will call and a valid military ID must be shown when picking up the tickets. The buyer must be active duty or retired military but additional tickets in the order may be used by anyone the buyer would like to bring.
