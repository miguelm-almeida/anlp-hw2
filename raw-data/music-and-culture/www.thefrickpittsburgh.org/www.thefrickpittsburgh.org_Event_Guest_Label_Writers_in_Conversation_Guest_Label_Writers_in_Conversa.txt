You’re invited to a lively and interactive evening where guest labelists share the insights behind the Kara Walker: Harper's Pictorial History of the Civil War (Annotated) works that inspired their labels. Engage in round-robin conversations and gain fresh perspectives on the art through their unique interpretations. It’s a rare chance to experience the art in an entirely new way!
