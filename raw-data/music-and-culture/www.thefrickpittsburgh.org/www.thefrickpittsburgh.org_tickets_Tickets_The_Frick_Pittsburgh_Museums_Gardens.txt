Kara Walker: Harper's Pictorial History of the Civil War (Annotated) comes to the Frick Art Museum from March 1 through May 25, 2025. Comprised of 15 large scale prints, the exhibition revisits the historical representations found in the two-volume anthology published in 1866, "Harper's Pictorial History of the Civil War."
