### Accomplishments and Priorities

### What Our Members Have to Say:

From the ever-expanding popularity of Picklesburgh, to the bustling outdoor dining scene, to dedicated services that keep our streets safe and clean, our mission is to foster a Downtown that is welcoming to all, and our members are vital part of everything we do.

Become a PDP Member  | Membership Directory

Current members can stay on top of member benefits, update contact information, download invoices and make membership payments – all in one place.

The PDP has a long history of providing innovative programs and services to improve and enhance Downtown’s business environment, which includes:

“The assistance provided by the Pittsburgh Downtown Partnership has been instrumental in our redevelopment efforts of the Union Trust Building. This has allowed us to recruit new tenants and several new restaurants to this historic Downtown landmark. Their research, data collection, and expertise help us to make a compelling case for investing in Downtown.”

“As naming sponsor of Light Up Night, Comcast has found the Pittsburgh Downtown Partnership to be a great asset and partner, an organization that works promptly and professionally to help us reach our goals and make the city an outstanding place to do business. The visibility we have achieved with PDP’s help and experience has exceeded our expectations and we look forwrad to continuing our relationship.”
